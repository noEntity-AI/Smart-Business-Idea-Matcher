# Smart Business Idea Matcher

A SaaS platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to find the right business idea or product based on their investment capacity.

##  Features
- Input budget, location (Global/Bangladesh), and business preferences
- Intelligent recommendations based on user input
- Recommendation includes:
  - Estimated investment required
  - Potential profit (ROI)
  - Risk level (low, medium, high)
  - Market demand (global & Bangladesh)
  - Short explanation for the idea match
- Clean, modern landing page
- Simple form for user inputs
- Results displayed in cards or lists
- Detailed view page for each business idea
- Mobile-friendly and beginner-friendly UI
- Optional AI-powered insights for smarter recommendations

## 🎯 Goal
To build a smart “investment-to-business idea matching platform” that helps users make better business decisions quickly and easily.
